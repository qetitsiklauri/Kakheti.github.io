Kobtavi Church

Kobtavi Church is located in Kakheti , Sagarejo Municipality , about  1.5-2 km away from the village of Paldo. The monument dates back to VIII-IX centuries. The church is a three-aisled basilica and is characterized by high, curved proportions. The temple is square in plan and does not have the elongated longitudinal axis characteristic of a basilica. The building shows traces of late repairs, during which bricks were used. The entrance is two, both - arched. The southern entrance was later lowered, while the northern one was rebuilt and only the lunette was left. The boats are separated from each other by a pair of piers and arches placed on them.

The piers are massive, rectangular in shape. The arches rest on the walls of the sanctuary on the east and the slightly sloping pilasters on the west. Imposts with a flat profile at the heels of the arches are separated. The middle boat is twice as tall and wide as the side boats. The main nave ends with a semicircular apse on the east and the sides without an apse. Pastoforiums are not isolated. The east and south walls of the church have one arched window cut, There are also windows on the east side boats. The boats are covered with cylindrical arches.
